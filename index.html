We provide washer, dryer and washing liquid. Laundry is in the basement, you can get there either through kitchen staircase or use bulkhead.

Firewood for stove and firepit is complimentary. Wood palette is in the basement, please use responsibly and let us know if we are running low.

We pick-up trash and recycle on Tuesday around noon. Please ensure all trash and recyclables are bagged, placed in the bins, and that the bins are accessible for collection.

Waterfront and Backyard

Dock, swim ladder,
boat

Fire Pit, free wood
adirondack chairs

Our house comes with a 7-foot fishing boat equipped with paddles and six life jackets (four for adults and two for children). The jackets and paddles are stored in the garage, and the door code is the same as the front door code. We also offer a boat motor rental for $200 (fule included). Lounge chairs, beach towels, and backyard games are available in the garage.
Our fire pit, with complimentary wood available in the basement, is surrounded by comfortable Adirondack chairs. It's the perfect spot to unwind in the evening with a glass of wine by the fire..
